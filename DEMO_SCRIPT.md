# ROBZEN Demo Script
### First Customer or Partner Conversation

---

## 1-Minute Intro (while sharing screen on the homepage)

> "ROBZEN solves one specific problem: German manufacturing SMEs want to automate, but the market is opaque, vendors have conflicts of interest, and a typical decision cycle takes 12 to 24 months. We cut that down to weeks.
>
> We're not selling robots. We're not taking vendor commissions. We help a Geschäftsführer go from 'I think we should automate something' to a concrete project profile with cost range, ROI orientation, and funding options — in under 15 minutes, for free.
>
> Let me show you how it works."

---

## 5-Minute Walkthrough

**Minute 1 — Homepage (30 sec)**
Scroll slowly through the hero and the 4-step process cards.

> "The whole value prop is on this page. Free, anonymous, no account. The four steps are exactly what we walk every customer through. Note the pricing section — the check and the profile are free. We only charge if a customer wants active brokering."

**Minute 2 — Automatisierungs-Check (90 sec)**
Click *Kostenlosen Check starten*. Fill it out live with a plausible scenario: a 3-shift palletising process, 8 workers, goal is fewer errors, budget €100k–250k.

> "Five questions, two minutes. Budget, team size, shift model, process type, goal. At the end they get a score."

Show the result page.

> "This score tells them where they stand — potential level, why they qualify for funding programs like go-digital or BAFA, and which use cases match their profile. Everything is flagged as indicative — we're not making promises, we're giving orientation."

**Minute 3 — KI-Intake with ZENI (90 sec)**
Navigate to /projekt-starten. Type "Hallo" and let ZENI ask the first question. Answer 2–3 questions live. Point to the steckbrief panel filling in on the right.

> "This is ZENI, our intake assistant. It asks nine questions, one at a time — the same questions a good consultant would ask in the first meeting. Watch the steckbrief panel on the right: it fills in live as the customer answers. No manual data entry, no PDF form."

**Minute 4 — Steckbrief + Cost View (60 sec)**
If you have a pre-filled demo steckbrief open at /projekt/[id], switch to it. Show the automation level slider and the cost/ROI block.

> "At the end the customer gets this. A structured project profile — process type, team size, investment range, payback horizon, funding eligibility. They can adjust the automation degree and see how the cost range shifts. They can also print or save this as a PDF."

**Minute 5 — Use Cases + Kontakt (30 sec)**
Quick pass through /use-cases, then /kontakt.

> "The use case catalog shows eight reference scenarios — cobot assembly, palletising, AMR transport, quality inspection — with real cost ranges and outcomes, all labeled as indicative. And the contact page is simple: form or WhatsApp, that's it."

## Key Value Points
*(Use these when questions come up — don't read them unprompted)*

- **Neutral by design.** No vendor commissions on purchasing decisions. Revenue only from project support if the customer opts in.
- **Speed.** A customer gets a structured project profile in 15 minutes. A traditional consultant takes 3 months to deliver the equivalent.
- **Funding visibility.** Many SMEs don't know they qualify for go-digital, BAFA, or regional programs. ROBZEN surfaces this automatically based on headcount and budget.
- **No lock-in.** Anonymous check, no account required. Reduces the barrier to the first conversation to zero.
- **AI-assisted, human-verified.** ZENI does the intake; a human advisor reviews and follows up. The AI speeds up the funnel, it doesn't replace the relationship.

## Questions to Ask the Customer

**On their situation:**
- "How many automation inquiries or projects are you currently evaluating — and how long has each been in the pipeline?"
- "Who in your organization is typically the bottleneck — the GF who needs to approve, the ops team who has to scope it, or the purchasing team who has to compare vendors?"

**On the platform fit:**
- "If a supplier came to you with a tool that handed them a pre-qualified project profile before the first call — how would that change your sales process?"
- "Would your customers be comfortable running through a 5-minute check themselves, or would this need to be done together in a meeting?"

**On trust and neutrality:**
- "What's your current process for comparing automation vendors? How confident are your customers that the recommendation they get is neutral?"

---

## Next-Step CTA

End every demo with one of these — pick based on who you're talking to:

**If it's a potential customer (SME):**
> "Would it make sense to run your actual process through the check right now? It takes five minutes and you'd leave with a real steckbrief for your team."

**If it's a partner or reseller:**
> "We're looking for two or three pilot partners in the DACH manufacturing space. The idea is you bring us into your existing customer conversations — we do the intake and scoping, you stay in the relationship. Want to explore what that looks like?"

**If it's an investor:**
> "We have the full intake flow live today. What we're building next is the provider network and the matching layer. Happy to walk you through the product roadmap — do you want to set up a follow-up this week?"

---

**Total demo time:** ~7 minutes including questions. Leave 5–10 minutes for conversation.
